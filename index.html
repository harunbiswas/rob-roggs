Marketing Retainer Overview

Our goal at Code Conspirators is to predictably and efficiently generate new leads for our marketing clients. Based on the volume and scope of work projected in the future, the most cost effective approach is a retainer agreement. Benefits of a retainer agreement include:

  • Reliable Budget - Establishing a finite number of marketing hours each month yields consistent billing and reliable budgeting.
  • Transparent Billing Structure - With a retainer in place, we provide you with a proof then payment billing structure. We will not exceed the agreed upon marketing hours each month without explicit approval in writing to do so.
  • Discounted Hourly Rates - Our rates under a retainer agreement are discounted, depending on the retainer option selected.
  • Retainer-Exclusive Benefits - In addition to our rate discounts, we offer other retainer- exclusive benefits not otherwise available, as noted in the table below.
